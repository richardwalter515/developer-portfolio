Workday Scheduler

This Workday Scheduler is designed to dynamically update throughout the day. When an hour of the day is grey, it is in the past. When it is red, it is currently happening, and when it is green it is in the future. When you enter text into an hour of the day and click "save," the text will remain there even if you navigate away from the Scheduler.

Weather Dashboard

This weather dashboard is designed to display the local weather conditions and five day forecast of any city you enter. The application uses the Open Weather Map API to provide accurate weather data. For each city you enter, a button will be created, and if you click on that button that city's weather forecast will re-appear.

Soundtrackify

Soundtrackify interacts with Spotify's API to provide a sountrack of songs based on a keyword entered by the user. The lyrics will be displayed to the right of the songs, and the user can click the 'add' button to open in Spotify. Note that this app requires the user to sign into their Spotify account.
